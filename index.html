Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new task?

To create a new task, tap the '+' button on the main screen. Enter the task details and tap 'Save'.

Can I customize categories?

Yes! Go to the 'Categories' tab and tap 'Add Category' to create your own custom categories.

How do I mark a task as complete?

Simply tap the circle next to a task to mark it as complete. Tap again to undo.
